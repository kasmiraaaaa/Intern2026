What we treat

Our services

From routine check-ups to chronic disease management, our team is here for every stage of your health journey.

General Medicine

Annual physicals, illness diagnosis, prescription management, and preventive screenings tailored to your age and risk profile.

Pediatric Care

Well-child visits, immunisations, developmental assessments, and sick-child appointments for newborns through adolescents.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing support for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and heart disease — with care plans built around your life, not just your labs.

Mental Health Support

Initial assessment, counselling referrals, and medication management for anxiety, depression, and stress-related conditions.

Vaccinations & Travel Health

Routine immunisations plus destination-specific vaccines and antimalarial prescriptions for international travel.

Telehealth Consultations

Video appointments available Monday through Saturday — same quality care from wherever you are.
